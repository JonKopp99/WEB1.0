  • Ender Dragon

    Final Boss

    The Ender Dragon is the final boss of Minecraft and one of the most difficult to slay. You must shoot down it's towers to weaken it, then shoot the dragon out of the sky. After defeating this terrible monster you unlock ender islands that contain lots of treasure and loot!

  • The Wither

    A Terrible Fow

    The Wither is a mob you hope to never cross paths with. He will kill you using his withering effect and his explosions. To kill him we reccomend locking him down deep within a cave and slaying him with a sword. To unlock this boss you will need 3 wither skeleton skulls that you can get from nether fortresses.

  • Elder Guardian

    A Terrible Fishy

    The Elder Guardian is a boss that protects some good loot! You must be careful when enterng his domain as he will shoot you with lasers that make it so you cannot break anything. To beat this boss you will need enchanted gear and a bunch of doors so that you can breathe under water.
